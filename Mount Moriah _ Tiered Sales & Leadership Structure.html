Mount Moriah — Tiered Sales & Leadership Structure

All targets must be achieved within one (1) year from the date of appointment or signing of the contract.

Broker / Owner

Commission: 6.5%

  • 150+ lots (team)
  • 25,000 sqm (team)
  • ₱5,000,000 commission (team)
  • Leads 3+ Branch Marketing Agents
Team Guidance:
Leads multiple Branch Marketing Agents (BMMs)

Branch Marketing Agent (BMM)

Commission: 6%

  • 80+ lots (team)
  • 12,000 sqm (team)
  • ₱2,000,000 commission (team)
  • Leads 2+ Sales Marketing Agents

Sales Marketing Agent (SMM)

Commission: 5.5%

  • 30+ lots (team)
  • 5,000 sqm (team)
  • ₱800,000 commission (team)
  • Leads 2+ Unit Marketing Agents

Unit Marketing Agent (UMM)

Commission: 5%

  • 10+ lots (team)
  • 1,500 sqm (team)
  • ₱200,000 commission (team)
  • Leads 2+ Recruitment Marketing Agents

Recruitment Marketing Agent (RMA)

Commission: 4.5%

  • 3-5 lots (team)
  • 1,000 sqm (team)
  • ₱100,000 commission (team)
  • Recruits 10+ accredited Sales Agents

Sales Agent

Commission: 4%

  • 1 lot (personal)
  • 200 sqm (personal)
  • Active in trainings, presentations, and trippings

Key Policies

  • Managers may have multiple downlines (2+ recommended).
  • Team production includes personal sales + all direct & indirect downlines.
  • Promotions require meeting targets and demonstrating leadership qualities.
Definitions:

"Team" refers to all direct and indirect downlines under a manager's supervision. All timelines are 1 year from the date of appointment/signing.
